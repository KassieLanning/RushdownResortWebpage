About the Resort

What is the Resort?

The Rushdown Resort is an Organization created by GoddessVi and Medikitt on July 27th, 2025

The organization originally started out on November 17th, 2024 as a tournament for the game Guilty Gear -Strive- titled "Mr. Badguy's Wild Ride"

The two only created an organization after seeking further tournaments and eventually sponsoring players

What does the Resort do?

As mentioned before the Rushdown Resort hosts tournaments, as well as sponsors some players to go to major FGC events such as Frosty Faustings and CEO
